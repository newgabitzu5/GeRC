Main Points

This persona, a tech-savy homeowner, has been chosen because he is part of the target audience that uses a smart personal assistant. For example :

  • For convenience, because this could allow him to control multiple appliances whilst even just passing by;
  • To save energy, because by using the personal assistant it would be much easier to manage everything from a single place;
  • Because the personal assistant would allow to add at any time, new gadgets at the touch of the finger and it would easily integrate and adapt with the rest of the appliances;
  • To have remote control over everything he owns.
Goals
  • Because he wants to simplify his daily routine and tasks by automating as many home appliances as they could;
  • Because he enjoys the fast pace at which new techologies develop and release and loves to explore them and use them to their fullest;
  • Because he loves the idea of customizing his appliances based on his routine, to fit his lifestyle best.
Frustrations and Pain Points

  • The possibility that not everything he would like to add on the app would be implemented;
  • Could potentially get really expensive;
  • Can't personalize everything he would like because of software limitations.

Name: Marc Jean

Background
  • Date of Birth: 14/10/1978
  • Gender: Male
  • Location: United States of America, Ohio
  • Work place: Unemployed
  • School: Kenyon College
  • Technology Level: None
Main Points

This persona is paralyzed in a wheelchair and has been chosen because is exactly the opposite of the target audience, not being able to use our application because:

  • The application revolves around gestures and the person cannot use its body if its fully paralyzed not being able to use the application at all;
  • Even if it is not fully paralyzed there is not a guarantee that he will be able to use all the gestures;
  • He is not able to move easily or sometimes at all, making a possibility for blind spots that will not detect the gestures.
Goals
  • The person wants to use the application but cannot because of its paralysis;
  • If the paralysis it’s not on the whole body it can be used for some gestures to make the person’s life a bit easier but still he will be able to use only a limited number of gestures.
Frustrations and Pain Points

  • Having movement problems the person could make a gesture in a way that it will not be correctly recognized by the application or not recognized at all;
  • The person could be in places that are not fully visible and would not be able to use the gestures due to the limited mobility.
